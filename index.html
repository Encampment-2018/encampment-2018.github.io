About

The Encampment is a long-standing Stake tradition that happens every four years. It’s an opportunity to gather the Young Men of the Stake to enjoy the challenges of high adventure and Boy Scout advancement under the direction and guidance of the Stake Presidency and Stake Young Men Presidency.

Lost Eden, AZ is located (at lat, lon) near Happy Jack, AZ. The 60 acre property is privately owned and has few improvements other than fencing and an ancient cabin. It’s covered with Ponderosa pine trees and meadow grasses and is surrounded by the Coconino National Forest. Camp is located about 10 miles from Blue Ridge Reservoir.
